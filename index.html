Overview

My project idea is a system to automate aquaponics, aquponics is a system where you farm fresh water fish and grow plants, the water with uneaten fish food and feces is used feed plants.The plants it inturn clean the water and its pumped back into he fish tank. I propose using a Rasberry Pi system too automate this process, through turing the hydoponic lights on during night time and activating pump to pump he freshwater back into the tank, tempreture control on the fish tank.also the gathering of A.I data to optemise yeilds.

Motivation

There are close to 8 billion people in the world and food is always going to be a major commodity especially in china which relies heavily on importing food. The resources of the world are finite and this system is a lot more efficient than standard aquaculture. In standard aquaculture they need to flush the waste water away too stop the nitrogen build up in the water.Thus this system can provide fish and plants for the demand of hungry humans too eat the AI application through using raspberry Pi will gather important data overtime too eventually create more yields with efficient use of finite resources like power and water.
